LampLighter

An Exploration and time management survival horror game in which you play as a lamplighter, exploring a haunted city, managing your resources and relying on lamps as your only source of safety.

Bitwise Key (in progress)

First Person 3 dimensional Mashup of the Roguelike and Metroidvania Genres. Player progression is tied to the acquisition of various abilities. Each new run, the player's abilities are reset, and the level randomizes the placement of the abilities and level geometry.
This is currently a solo project, in which I've been the sole programmer to implement AI behavior through state trees, the randomization mechanics, and character movement controls.
